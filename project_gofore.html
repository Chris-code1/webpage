Virtual Meeting Room Redesign

Create the Virtual meeting room of the future! Redesign of the room by applying the Design Sprint 2.0.

The objective of my time at GoFore was to redesign the virtual meeting room. On arrival the initial design was a table and chairs on which all the participants were seated. After applying the Design Spring 2.0 the meeting Room looked the following.

The canvas in the middle of the screen asks the player to scan a student card. Once the card is scanned the animated workers appear (instructions phase). After that it asks to scan a folded 5 Euro bill, which leads to President Macron appearing on top of it. Following the player can then shot the workers by moving the 5 Euro bill in his hand.

Once all workers are eliminated, a success panel pops up, which rates the time needed to reform the workers. The player is offered to play again by pressing a button on the canvas.
