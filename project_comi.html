Comi - Hi-Fi prototype for a cooking assistant mobile app

Group project developed during the course of a User Experience and Mobile Interaction course. Of course before creating the hifi prototype, interviews, personas, task scenarios, storyboards, user journey map, moodboards and Lo-Fi prototypes were created :))

If you prefer to touch rather than read, test the Hifi prototype --->here!<----

For the creation we followed the Human Centered Design process. Interviews were conducted, and an empathy map, and a customer journey map were the result, also inspired by the value proposition design book .

Once the needs and our customer was well understood we moved straight to creating a hifi prototype in Figma. Before that a moodboard was created to agree on colors, fonts and styles. Also we thought about how to target the moment of truth that we identified in our app, which would be the moment when someone cooked and it did not turn out as on the fancy internet pictures.
